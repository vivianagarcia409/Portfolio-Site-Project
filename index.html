about me

Having graduated from Florida International University at 23 with a degree in Psychology, I had a world of options at my feet. I began working as a front office/student affairs coordinator for a non-profit private organization for children with autism and related disabilities and found my love in the creative realm. With working in administration, I found myself designing flyers and invitations with everyday applications and wanted to expand my knowledge in the subject.
With that being said, my full stack developing skills began at Ironhack. I advanced a hobby into a career by helping clients bring their vision to life with websites and/or applications specially curated for their intended viewers. Allow me do the same for you!
